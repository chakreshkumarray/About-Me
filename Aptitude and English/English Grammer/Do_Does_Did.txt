Do, DOes, Did :

Present Indefinite --> Do, Does

Past Indefinite --> Did

Negative & Interrogative Sentences

Present Indefinite -- > Do , Does
I      |
We     |
You    |---> Do
They   |
Plural |

He            |
She           |
It            |---> Does
Singular Noun |

Rule: Subject + Hepling Verb + (not) + Main Verb + Object

Ex: Main Vahan jata hoon.
    I go there.

Ex: Main vahan nhi jata hoon.
    I do not go there.

Ex: Vah vahan jati hai.
    She goes there.

Ex: Vah vahan nhi jati hai.
    She does not go there.

Ex: Rohan vahan nahi jata.
    Rohan does not go there.

Ex: Rohan aur Meera vahan nahi jate.
    Rohan and Meera do not go there.


Past Indefinite -- > Did
I             |
We            |
You           |
They          |
Plural Noun   |-----> Did
He            |
She           |
It            |
Singular Noun |

Rule: Subject + Helping Verb + (not) + Main Verb + Object

Ex: Main vahan gai thi.
    I went there.

Ex: Main waha nhi gai thi.
    I did not go there.

Ex: Vah vahan gai thi.
    She went there.

Ex: Vah vahan nhi gai thi.
    She did not go there.

Ex: Meera vahan nhi gai thi.
    Meera did not go there.

Ex: Rohan aur Meera vahan nahi gaye the.
    Rohan and Meeera did not go there

Ex: Ve Roj nhi khelte hain. 
    They do not play everyday.

Ex: Log parvaah nhi karte the.
    People did not care.

Ex: Kisi ne kuch nhi kiya.
    Nobody did anything.
    Anybody did not do anything.

Interrogative Sentence

Rule: Helping Verb + Subject + (not) + Main Verb + Object

Ex: Kya ap tennis khelte hain?
    Do you play tennis.

Ex: Kya ap kahaniyan sunte the?
    Did you listen to stories.

Ex: Kya aap khidkiyaan nahi todte hain?
    Do you not break windows?

Ex: Kya aapne khaya nahi?
    Did you not eat?

Interrogative Sentence:

Rule: (Question Word) + Helping Verb + (not) + Main Verb + Object

Ex: Riya kaha kaam karti h ?
    Where does riya work.

Ex: Tum kaha kaam karte the?
    Where did you work.

Do, Does, Did

To add emphasis(Forcefully)

Ex: Main vahan jata hoon.
    I go there.
    I do go there.

Ex: Vah vahan jati hai.
    She goes there.
    She does go there.

Ex: Vo vahan gai thi.
    She went there.
    She did go there.

Ex: Hum wo karte hain.
    We do that.
    We do do that.

Ex: Maine wo kiya tha.
    I did that.

Ex: Maine us se baat ki thi.
    I did talk to her.

Ex: मैं कल आपको कॉल किया था
    I called you yesterday.

Ex: मैंने कल आपसे फोन पर बात थी
    I spoke with you on the phone yesterday.

Ex: आप वहा  गए थे क्या 
    Did you go there?

Ex: कल आपका कॉल  था 
    You called yesterday.
