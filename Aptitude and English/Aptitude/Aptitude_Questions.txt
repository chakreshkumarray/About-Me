Q2. The difference between ci and si on a sum for 3 years at 10 percent percent annum in INR 93 . The sum is?
Ans. Given:
Difference between CI and SI for 3 years = 93
Rate = 10% per annum

Formula:
Difference = P × (R/100)^2 × (3 + R/100)

Substitute values:
93 = P × (10/100)^2 × (3 + 10/100)

93 = P × (0.1)^2 × (3 + 0.1)

93 = P × 0.01 × 3.1

93 = P × 0.031

P = 93 / 0.031

P = 3000

✅ Final Answer: 3000

Question 3: Pipes and Cistern

A cistern has two pipes. Pipe A can fill it in 15 minutes and Pipe B can empty it in 20 minutes.
 If both are opened alternately for 1 minute each, starting with A, how long will it take to fill the cistern?

Assume total capacity = LCM of 15 and 20 = 60 units
Pipe A efficiency = 60 / 15 = 4 units per minute
Pipe B efficiency = 60 / 20 = 3 units per minute (emptying)

In 2 minutes (A then B), net fill = 4 - 3 = 1 unit

To avoid overfilling:
60 - 4 = 56 units

1 unit takes 2 minutes
56 units take 56 x 2 = 112 minutes

At the 113th minute, Pipe A adds 4 units:
56 + 4 = 60 units

✅ Cistern is filled in 113 minutes

Question 4: 
      How many 4-digit numbers can be formed using digits {0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5} such that the number is divisible by 3 and repetition is not allowed?

1. Divisibility Rule
Sum of digits must be a multiple of 3

{1, 2, 4, 5} (sum = 12)
{0, 3, 4, 5} (sum = 12)
{0, 2, 3, 4} (sum = 9)
{0, 1, 3, 5} (sum = 9)
{0, 1, 2, 3} (sum = 6)

Set without 0:
4! = 24

Sets with 0 (4 sets):
First digit cannot be 0 -> 3 choices
Remaining 3 digits arranged in 3! = 6 ways
Total per set = 3 x 6 = 18

Total = 18 x 4 = 72

✅ 24 + 72 = 96

Question 13: Boats and Streams

Question:
A man rows at 6 km/h in still water. The current speed is 2 km/h. He takes 3 hours to go and return. Find the distance.

Answer: 8 km

Step-by-Step Solution:

1. Speeds:
Downstream = 8 km/h
Upstream = 4 km/h

2. Equation:
d/8 + d/4 = 3

3. Solve:
(d + 2d) / 8 = 3
3d / 8 = 3
3d = 24
d = 8 km

Question 14: Algebraic Identity

Question:
If x + 1/x = 3, find x^5 + 1/x^5

Answer: 123

Step-by-Step Solution:

1. x^2 + 1/x^2:
= (x + 1/x)^2 - 2
= 9 - 2 = 7

2. x^3 + 1/x^3:
= (x + 1/x)^3 - 3(x + 1/x)
= 27 - 9 = 18

3. Multiply:
7 x 18 = 126

4. Identity:
(x^2 + 1/x^2)(x^3 + 1/x^3)
= (x^5 + 1/x^5) + (x + 1/x)

5. Final:
126 - 3 = 123

Question 15: Clock Angles

Question:
At what time between 4 and 5 o'clock are the hands of a clock at right angles?

Answer:
5 5/11 minutes past 4
38 2/11 minutes past 4

Step-by-Step Solution:

1. Initial angle:
At 4:00 -> 4 x 30 = 120 degrees

2. Required angle:
90 degrees

3. Case 1:
120 - 90 = 30
Time = 30 / 5.5 = 60/11 = 5 5/11 minutes

4. Case 2:
120 + 90 = 210
Time = 210 / 5.5 = 420/11 = 38 2/11 minutes
